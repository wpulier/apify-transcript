# Apify Store Listing

## Title

Large Video to Transcript

## Short Description

Upload large video or audio files and get an MP3 plus timestamped transcript exports with speaker labels, subtitles, JSON, ZIP, and quality reports.

## Positioning

Turn long recordings into reusable files without local setup. Upload MP4, MOV, WebM, MP3, M4A, or WAV files and receive a normalized MP3, readable transcript, subtitle exports, structured JSON, a quality report, and a ZIP bundle.

## Lead Benefits

- No local ffmpeg or transcription setup.
- Handles large recordings through an Apify Actor run.
- Downloads MP3, TXT, JSON, SRT, VTT, quality report, and ZIP.
- Built for client calls, Zoom recordings, webinars, podcasts, and long meeting videos.
- Outputs clean dataset rows with browser-download links.
